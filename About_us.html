Welcome to Turtle Trax

About Us

Turtle Trax is a dedicated team committed to the well-being and conservation of turtles in Sri Lanka. Our platform serves as a gathering place for turtle aficionados, conservationists, and concerned citizens who are united in their desire to safeguard these lovely creatures and emphasize their value in our ecosystem.

We are lucky to be located on the beautiful island of Sri Lanka, surrounded by spectacular natural beauty and rich marine life. Turtles, in particular, have a specific role in nature's delicate balance. They are not only intriguing creatures, but they also provide information about the health of our seas and coastal ecosystems.

Turtle Trax believes it is our responsibility to protect these lovely beings. We hope to increase awareness about the threats turtles face through our activities, such as habitat destruction, pollution, climate change, and illegal trading. By bringing these challenges to light, we hope to motivate action and promote a better understanding of the interconnectivity of all living things.

Our team is made up of dedicated researchers, conservationists, educators, and volunteers who work relentlessly to make a difference. To create and implement effective conservation plans, we work with local communities, groups, and government agencies. We are actively involved in every element of turtle protection, from research and rescue operations to educational initiatives and beach clean-ups.

We offer a multitude of resources, instructional materials, and entertaining content on our website to encourage people to become turtle conservation activists. We provide chances for people from all walks of life to actively contribute to our purpose through volunteering, fundraising, and hands-on experiences.

Our Vision

Our vision is to establish a sustainable future for turtles in Sri Lanka. We imagine a world in which these amazing species can thrive in their natural environments, free of human intervention. We hope to establish a happy coexistence between humans and turtles by promoting a profound understanding and admiration for turtles among local residents and visitors alike, ensuring their survival for future generations.

Our Mission

Our mission is to actively safeguard and promote turtle welfare in Sri Lanka. We hope to have a positive impact on turtle populations through education, conservation, and community engagement. We are dedicated to exploring creative solutions to challenges to their survival, such as habitat deterioration, pollution, and poaching. We hope to establish a society that recognizes and respects these unique species by pushing for sustainable behaviors and cultivating a sense of environmental responsibility.

Join us at Turtle Trax as we embark on this important journey to safeguard the future of turtles in Sri Lanka. Together, we can make a significant difference in protecting and preserving these magnificent creatures and the natural heritage they represent.
